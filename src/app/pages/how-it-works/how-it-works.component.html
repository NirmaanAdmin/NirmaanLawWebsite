How it works

3 step process to get set and ready to go.

Get started

Managing Construction Projects has never been this easier
1 — Sign up You will receive an email ID and Password to sign in the platform from  Nirmaan360's backend team after you sign up either for a demo/paid version/enterprise version.
Get started
2 — Create a Project Sign in to the platform and explore the demo project. After understanding basic functioning of the platform - Create your Own Project(s) and Add Stakeholders (under Project Contacts module) with whom you want to contribute in the project.
3 — Explore Features After creating projects and adding stakeholders collaborate seamlessly with them by using tools like Task Management, Inventory Control, Daily Progress Reporting, Labour Management, and Payment Tracking.

Our features

Nirmaan360 is an all-in-one construction management suite for your tablet, phone or computer. With plans starting at  ₹ 4250/month , it is the most affordable management software for real estate stakeholders.
