Frequently asked questions

The fee listed on the website is the fee for the company as a whole. Any current promotion will be automatically factored into the price.

Scheduling a demo meeting with us is a great way to see how easy it is to use the system and to make sure it will meet your needs. During this time you’ll be able to ask us any questions and see it in action. Schedule your demo here:  https://calendly.com/nirmaan360/30min

Nirmaan360 is web based as of now. The web panel can be used on any device that has a web browser. We support Firefox and Chrome. Safari and Edge are not recommended but they normally work without issue. If you are using one of those and do have an issue, try a different browser.

Nirmaan360 works in the cloud on Microsoft Azure. Do not share your credentials – instead, give your employees their own account.

You are only charged at the end of the quarter or at the end of the year based on your plan. To cancel, your COMPANY ADMIN will need to do so before your upcoming renewal through your account here by submitting the cancellation form (Profile Settings > Cancel Subscription).
